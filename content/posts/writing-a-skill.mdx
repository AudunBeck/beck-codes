Writing has been something I quite didn't see the value of while growing up. It was something that only *writers* and authors did.

Over the last few years I keep seeing how valuable it is to center ones thoughts. It started when I had an Obsidian craze, were I diligently took notes before, after and during meeting. People quickly thought I had been taking notes for *years*.

I kept it up for some time, but did see that it would be hard to bring along a laptop everywhere. So I got a Traveler's Notebook, and a Lamy fountain pen. Starting to take physical notes that I then rewrote to digital ones, before sharing with those in interest of it.
